Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-13

Career Coach is an open-source browser extension maintained by an individual, not a company. This policy is a plain-language, good-faith description of how data flows through it — not a substitute for legal advice.

Default vs. self-hosted: unless you follow the self-hosting steps in the README to deploy your own Supabase project, the extension as cloned/loaded connects to the maintainer's Supabase project by default. That means your data is stored on shared infrastructure the maintainer operates, not infrastructure only you control, unless you deploy your own.

What we collect

  • Account info — email and password, handled by Supabase Auth. Passwords are hashed; the maintainer never sees them in plain text.
  • Resume text — whatever you paste into the Resume tab or extract from an uploaded PDF. Scanned/image-only PDFs are rejected before extraction because they are not ATS-safe.
  • Captured and imported job postings — the URL, title, company, location, and job text of roles you save or manually add to Discovery.
  • Application workspace data — status, notes, follow-up dates, generated artifacts, application packets, submission notes, and preference feedback you enter.
  • Interview and planning data — story-bank entries, practice answers, prep checklist state, weekly goals, reminders, and retrospectives.

How it's used

Your resume text and a captured job's text are sent to the server-selected AI provider solely to generate tailored materials and scoring for that job. Uploaded text-layer PDFs may also be sent to the temporary extraction provider until parser-first PDF extraction replaces that fallback. These calls happen server-side — your browser never holds an AI provider's API key or chooses a raw model. We don't use your data for anything beyond the product workflow, and we don't sell it.

Sending your resume/job text to a third-party AI provider means that provider's own privacy policy may also apply to that data: Anthropic, OpenAI, Google.

Where it's stored

In a Postgres database hosted by Supabase, with row-level security scoped to your account — enforced at the database level, not just in the app's UI, so no other user's queries can reach your rows.

Deleting your data

There's no self-service "delete my account" button in the extension yet — that's an honest gap, not a hidden decision. Until it exists, open a GitHub issue or contact the maintainer directly and your account and its data will be deleted manually.

Changes

If this policy changes in a way that matters, the "Last updated" date above will change and, where practical, we'll note what changed.
